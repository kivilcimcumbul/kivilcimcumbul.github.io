Ethics & Policies

Social Responsibilities



As ULAC GROUP, we aim to continue our activities with our employees, customers and suppliers in the requirements of the age, honesty and trust.


We present our company policy to all our business partners in our production facility with a social awareness, and we work primarily in accordance with our laws, production procedures and social standards of our customers and invite them to become partners in this understanding.

• ULAC GROUP has adopted the following items as a principle in line with the company policy:

• At ULAC GROUP, the working age is as determined by law, and child labor is not allowed or supported.

• ULAC GROUP does not employ company employees under any pressure. All employees are employed in suitable positions with their consent and under equal conditions.

• ULAC GROUP fulfills the legal requirements for the health and safety of all its employees, takes the necessary measures to prevent all the health and safety risks of the personnel, and supports their development and training opportunities and ensures occupational health and safety.

• ULAC GROUP employees' working environment is well-equipped and offers every opportunity to meet their basic needs in hygienic conditions.

• ULAC GROUP employees are free to establish collective agreements and unions.

•Employees at ULAC GROUP have freedom of opinion, belief, and no discrimination is applied to anyone such as language, religion, race, gender, social class, age and union membership.

•ULAC GROUP regulates working hours as prescribed by law, does not regularly work overtime and gives employees two days a week.

•ULAC GROUP wage policy is evaluated according to our employees' knowledge and skills and no payment is made below the minimum wage.

•Employees at ULAC GROUPreport their requests and complaints to the management through employee representatives and their solutions are analyzed and concluded by management.

•ULAC GROUP does not discipline any of its employees according to physical or material discipline rules and ensures the development of its employees with training and instructions.

•ULAC GROUP fulfills its social responsibility for environmental pollution and protection of nature and raises awareness of its employees in this regard.

•ULAC GROUP controls all the criteria in company policy with social management and quality management systems and manages with a modern management system and aims to continue its activities with all its suppliers and business partners.



Product Safety Policies



As ULAC GROUP, we implement the following activities to ensure the safety of our products in the areas of use:


Effective combat against pests and pests:

• All complaints about pests and pests that may come from our employees are taken into consideration.

• Continues the management support to provide measures that can be taken by evaluating the demands from our customers.

• In order to ensure product safety during production, storage and shipment stages and to contribute to the product safety measures of our customers, periodic field controls are planned, measures are taken for the safety of the areas with the support of technical experts.



Jewelry Usage:

• Watch, rings, earrings, etc. of the personnel working in the production areas. Precautions are taken not to use jewelry and clothing that may compromise the safety of the product.



Foreign Matter Policy:

• Cracked and broken glass, plastic, etc. within the production area. It is ensured that materials are not available, except for those that are mandatory to use.

• The personnel in the field are responsible for the safe use of the materials in the area, and if these materials are damaged, they are removed from the area by intervening as soon as possible.


Resource needs for the measures to be taken are provided with the support of the General Manager. The effectiveness of the measures taken under the responsibility of the management representative is evaluated.
